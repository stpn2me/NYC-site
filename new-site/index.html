How it works

Using NewYorkCoin is not that complicated

  • Download the Wallet

    Download NYC Wallet app for your PC, Android, or iOS to create, send and receive NewYorkCoins. You can create multiple wallets for one account.

  • Add NewYorkCoin to Your Wallet

    Once the app is downloaded you can add the NYC you’ve already created or exchanged through an online exchange.

  • Send/Receive with Wallet

    The process of sending NYC is very easy. In two words, you need to enter receiver's NYC address, specify the amount, confirm address and send.
